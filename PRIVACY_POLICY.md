# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 7, 2026**

Feed My Dog: AI Meal Planner ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

Feed My Dog: AI Meal Planner is a local-first application. All pet data, feeding schedules, and preferences are stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

If you enable iCloud Sync, your data is synced through your personal Apple iCloud account. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

## 2. Notifications & Calendar

If you grant permission, Feed My Dog: AI Meal Planner uses local notifications to remind you of feeding schedules. Calendar events are created in your device's calendar app. These features are entirely on-device.

## 3. Analytics

We may collect anonymized, non-personally-identifiable usage analytics to improve app performance. No personal data or pet information is included.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Feed My Dog: AI Meal Planner does not share any data with third-party services, advertisers, or data brokers.

## 5. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. Deleting the app removes all stored data. iCloud data can be managed through your Apple ID settings.

## 6. AI-Generated Content

Feed My Dog: AI Meal Planner uses artificial intelligence to generate feeding suggestions, meal plans, care recommendations, and other pet-related content. All AI-generated content is processed and stored locally on your device. No pet data is sent to external AI services. For full details on how AI-generated content is handled and its limitations, please refer to our [AI Disclaimer](AI_DISCLAIMER.md).

## 7. User Responsibility for Data

You are solely responsible for all data you enter, store, or manage within Feed My Dog: AI Meal Planner. This includes pet profiles, health records, feeding schedules, images, and any other information. We do not verify, validate, or review the accuracy of any data you input. It is your responsibility to ensure that the information you rely on within the app is correct and up to date. We are not liable for any consequences arising from inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated data that you have entered.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Feed My Dog: AI Meal Planner does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed as a general-purpose pet care tool.

## 9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@feedmydog.app.
