## Common Topics

### App Features
- Pet profile management (add, edit, delete dogs)
- Meal logging and feeding history
- Recurring feeding schedules with reminders
- Food safety checker (94 foods with safety levels)
- Vaccine timeline tracking
- Care reminders (water, wash, play, vet visits)

### Subscriptions
- Manage your subscription via [Apple Subscription Settings](https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions)
- Plans available: Weekly, Monthly, Yearly, Lifetime

### Privacy
- All data is stored locally on your device
- No data is sent to external servers
